# Competitive Analysis: Renamerly vs Renamify

**Last Updated:** April 15, 2026  
**Competitor:** [Renamify.co](https://renamify.co)

---

## 📊 Executive Summary

Renamify is our primary competitor in the AI-powered image renaming space. They position themselves as a "bulk file renaming" tool with AI suggestions, currently pricing at **$10/month** for their Starter Plan.

**Key Findings:**
- Renamify has **broader file support** (not just images)
- They emphasize **AI-powered suggestions** (99% accuracy claim)
- Their pricing is **$10/month** for premium features
- They offer **photo album organization**
- Strong privacy messaging (no storage/training)

**Our Positioning Opportunity:** 
We should focus on **e-commerce specialization** and **product photography workflows** rather than competing on general file renaming.

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

### **1. Pricing Strategy**

**Recommendation: $9/month** (undercut by $1)

**Rationale:**
- Renamify charges $10/month
- We offer **more specialized features** for e-commerce
- $9/month is **psychologically attractive** ($9.00 vs $10.00)
- Still profitable with Stripe fees (~$0.56/transaction)
- Positions us as the **value leader** in e-commerce photo management

**Alternative: $12/month**
- If we can demonstrate **2x the value** with e-commerce features
- Only viable if we add Shopify/Etsy integrations
- Premium positioning vs. general-purpose tool

**My Recommendation: Start at $9/month, increase to $12 when integrations launch**
